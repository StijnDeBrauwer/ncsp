PARTS FOR CROWN-BAELE MACHINES

NEW CROWN is the exclusive and sole original manufacturer of spare parts and assemblies for all CROWN-BAELE machines. Every enquiry for spare parts goes through the hands of our engineering team. Manufacturing methods change and evolve continuously, and we make sure the parts are made according to the latest standards in terms of quality, durability and safety.

PARTS FOR THIRD PARTY MACHINES

Throughout the history, many companies where related to or even part of the CROWN group for a while, such as Ortmann & Herbst, Kettner, Gangloff and others. NEW CROWN therefore owns the drawings and technical specifications of a large number of parts for machines of these brands and can offer these at very interesting conditions.

REVERSE ENGINEERED PARTS

NEW CROWN offers services and maintenance on machines of all brands, which makes reverse engineering necessary. By reverse engineering parts of third-party equipment, we analyze eventual weak points and improve the execution of the part. Not only to improve durability or sustainability, but also to make maintenance easier, or more cost effective.
