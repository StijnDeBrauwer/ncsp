NEW CROWN offers solutions to modernize and upgrade existing Returnable Glass Bottling equipment, not only to restore its nominal capacity, but also to meet all current standards in terms of sustainability, quality and safety.

THE ORIGINS OF THE CROWN

NEW CROWN Service Partners NV was founded in October 2017 by two former employees and acquired the intellectual property and trade fund of CROWN-BAELE, who was declared bankrupt a month before. This makes NEW CROWN the sole official OEM of CROWN-BAELE parts and assemblies.

They took the name NEW CROWN to mark the inseparable connection with the history of its illustrious predecessor yet indicating a new business model and ambition in becoming the reference for upgrading older equipment in the global RGB industry.

STRONG TEAM

The NCSP team is a great mix of seasoned experience and young enthusiasm, of proven solutions and new technologies. We offer global services, in Belgium, in Europe and outside, with a special knack for Africa, where we have a longtime experience, and where we know we can make a difference.

STRONG PARTNERSHIP

The ownership of NEW CROWN Service Partners NV is held by business partners Thomas Bernolet and Verney Poynton, and by strategic partner LAMBRECHTS GROUP. Lambrechts Group has a long and successful track record as an equipment manufacturer of kegging lines and industrial washing installations in the brewery industry. The many synergies between New Crown Service Partners and Lambrechts Group strengthens their positions on the market.
