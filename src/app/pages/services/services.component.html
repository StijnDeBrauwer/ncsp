MAINTENANCE

In order to keep a machine in good working order, it is not sufficient to make a revision once or twice a year. Maintenance is a daily duty. During our interventions we always take the time to give your people a thorough explanation about the maintenance required and explain to them what needs to be done and when. By always doing this with your people hands-on, we do not only teach them what to do, but they also understand why, and what the consequences of the lack of maintenance can be.
